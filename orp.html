"Every orphan deserves love, care, and a future filled with hope."

Anadha Seva

✅Providing a safe and loving home for orphaned children.

✅Ensuring basic necessities like food, clothing, and healthcare.

✅Creating a family-like atmosphere where children feel emotionally secure.

Vedic & Moral Education

✅Teaching Hindu scriptures like the Bhagavad Gita, Upanishads, and Ramayana.

✅Instilling moral values like truthfulness, compassion, and self-discipline.

✅Conducting daily prayers and storytelling sessions on Sanatana Dharma principles.

Yoga & Meditation for Mental Strength

✅Conducting daily Yoga sessions to improve concentration and physical fitness.

✅Teaching meditation techniques to help children develop emotional stability.

✅Promoting holistic wellness through breath control (Pranayama) and mindfulness.

Spiritual Growth & Bhakti Yoga

✅Encouraging daily Bhajans, Kirtans, and temple visits for spiritual connection.

✅Teaching meditation and chanting of Vedic mantras for inner peace.

✅Organizing religious discourses to deepen their understanding of Dharma.

Healthcare & Ayurveda for Well-being

✅Providing free healthcare facilities, including regular checkups.

✅Using Ayurvedic treatments and natural remedies for physical health.

✅Offering mental health support through Yoga and meditation.
