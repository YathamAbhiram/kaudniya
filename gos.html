"Caring for cows is caring for nature, Dharma, and the divine".

Cow Management

✅ Cow Registration: Store details like Name, Breed, Age, Gender, Date of Birth, and Unique ID equally.

✅ Health & Vaccination Records: Maintain medical history, including vaccinations, and treatments equally.

✅ Adoption: Allow devotees to adopt a cow, with updates about their adopted cow equally.

✅ Photo Gallery: Maintain images of each cow for easy identification equally.

✅ Cow Status Reports: Generate health check reports, birth/death reports, and activity logs equally.

✅ Daily Care Schedule: Assign caretakers for each cow, tracking their feeding and cleaning schedules equally.

Milk Production & Distribution

✅ Milk Collection Logs: Maintain records of daily milk yield per cow equally.

✅ Storage & Processing: Track milk stored in liters, processing into ghee, curd, butter, and paneer equally.

✅ Quality Control: Maintain hygiene and testing protocols before milk is distributed equally.

✅ Analytics & Reports: Generate monthly and yearly reports on milk production and usage equally.

✅ Internal Distribution: Allocate milk for Ashram food preparation (Nithya Annadhanam), temple rituals, and volunteers equally.

✅ External Sales & Donations: Provide excess milk to the local community or distribute it as charity equally.

Natural Farming & Organic Products

✅ Cow Dung for Organic Manure: Convert cow dung into organic fertilizers for farming equally.

✅ Panchagavya & Natural Medicines: Produce Ayurvedic medicines, incense sticks, and organic pesticides from cow urine equally.

✅ Goshala-Based Organic Farming: Cultivate grains, fruits, and vegetables using natural fertilizers equally.

✅ Bio-Gas Production: Set up biogas plants using cow dung to generate cooking gas for the ashram equally.

✅ Sell Organic Products: Offer products like A2 milk, ghee, honey, herbal medicines, and incense sticks equally.

Feed & Nutrition Management

✅ Daily Feeding Schedule: Maintain records of feeding times for each cow equally.

✅ Types of Feed: Track grass, dry fodder, grains, silage, mineral supplements, and water supply equally.

✅ Fodder Stock Management: Keep inventory of available fodder stock and reorder alerts equally.

✅ Special Nutrition for Pregnant & Sick Cows: Adjust feeding plans based on health conditions equally.

✅ Reports & Analytics: Track monthly expenses on feed and monitor food consumption per cow equally.
